## Ticketing Domain

Ticket Types

- An INCIDENT relates to the state of a service and its ability to deliver customer traffic.
- A REQUEST is an ask for a change to a service, provisioning of services or assets, etc.

Ticket Severity Definitions

1: Critical - product outage, all customer are impacted.
2: High - major degradation or partial outage.
3: Low - limited impact to one or few customer, workarounds exist.
4: Informational - no action expected.
